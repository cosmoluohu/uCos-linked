Protocols for Handshank V2.0:


///////////////////////////////Vibrations/////////////////////////////////////
START:        USART_RX_BUF[0] = 0xFF    
MODE:         USART_RX_BUF[1] = 0x0A, 0x0B, 0x0C , 0x0F   //Never 0D !
ID/way/XX:    USART_RX_BUF[2] = 0x01-0x05  or 0x01,0x02 or 0x0A5:all vibrating!
Dir:          USART_RX_BUF[3] = 0x01,0x02,
SpeedLevel:   USART_RX_BUF[4] = 0x01-0x05 -> (0x01-0x05)*111 = 111-555
Time/Period:  USART_RX_BUF[5] = 0x00-0xFF -> (0x01-0xFF)*100 = 100-25600 ms  
              USART_RX_BUF[5] = 0 ms  for time means  infinite rotating
END:          USART_RX_BUF[6] = 0xEE 
So, there are 7 bytes to be sent in commands.

usart_end_mark1:    USART_RX_BUF[7] = 0x0d
usart_end_mark2:    USART_RX_BUF[8] = 0x0a  
      these two should be the "back space"
and this should be same as the following protocol.

eg, FF0A0101050CEE
means to select motor 1 to vibrate with a time 13*100ms.
eg, FF0B01010305EE
means to vibrate upflow with a time 5*100ms.
eg, FF0B0203050FEE
means to vibrate downflow with a time 15*100ms.
eg, FF0CA5010508EE
means to select all motors to vibrate with a time 8*100ms.
////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

//////////////////////Softness////////////////////////////////////////////
FFAAxx010203EE
where,xx should be from 0x00-0x09,
0x00 means softness grade_0(0 Volt output, to stop the serve-valve);
0x09 means softness grade_9(almost 1.06 Volts output ).
////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

///////////////////////Peltier///////////////////////////////////////
FFBB0101AABBEE    开冷
FFBB0202AABBEE    开热
FFBB0000AABBEE    停开！
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

///////////////to connect Pin TX6 & RX6 and the GND /////////////////
///////////////////////Texture///////////////////////////////////////
FFCCxx020304EE
where,xx should be from 0x01,0x03
eg, FFCC01020304EE
means you want to have an activated texture memberane 
eg, FFCC03020304EE
means you want to stop activate texture memberane
